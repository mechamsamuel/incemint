Elevating the ICO Landscape for Smarter Tokensale Participation

The Smart ICO Escrow Service that Brings Confidence to Your ICO Participation

Welcome to ICO 2.0: Bringing Issuers and Buyers Together Confidently

Incremint will provide an escrow solution that strengthens the ICO market for issuers and empowers token buyers. Using smart contract technology, the Incremint solution transfers a portion of the control from the token issuer to the token buyers, protecting buyers from undeveloped ICOs that may never mature into a completed project. Incremint will offer token buyers the opportunity to vote on whether or not to authorize a release of funds escrowed from the ICO. In addition, ICOs will have a ready-to-go community of active participants that can be a foundation for a great raise. As such, Incremint will increase demand for the tokens of issuers and simultaneously mitigate risks for token buyers.

Introducing MINT, the Token Created for Future ICO Participation

In addition to building a community dedicated to the advancement of blockchain and ICO technologies, Incremint will also introduce MINT tokens that provide both short- and long-term benefits to token holders. In the short-term, token buyers gain access to the Incremint community, which comes with authority for escrow voting and news of upcoming ICO tokensale openings. In the long-term, token holders will continue to receive information regarding vetted, approved tokensales, and will have built-in 10% discounts when using MINTs to make a purchase in an ICO. MINT tokens provide more control, more savings, and more confidence in future ICO investments.
